# Select fantasy premises by causal contribution

A candidate load-bearing fantasy premise is eligible for provisional adoption only when it has a stated causal route into political scale and at least one other epic channel. These routes begin as evidence-graded claims, not achieved outcomes, and adoption remains provisional until the claims survive tests permitted by their evidence ceilings. If the additional channel is narrative or spectacular rather than emotional, the design must explicitly identify what other core structure is expected to produce emotional scale.

The premise's asymmetries and exceptions must be learnable within the complexity budget. Fantasy enabling grammar may reduce their complexity cost; where the rendering supplies no such grammar, the premise pays its full teaching, attention, and evaluation cost.

The fantasy rendering must causally explain multiple consequences and help players anticipate them better than a steelmanned mundane rendering. Comparative teaching or inference tests should measure unsupported rules and rule-inference errors rather than reward a superficial noun-swap. This test can eliminate mundane renderings, but it cannot choose epic fantasy over equally explanatory horror or science fiction: epic fantasy is a declared product constraint among the surviving speculative renderings, not an empirical result of this test.

## Evaluation protocol

Evaluation cost rises with the decision at stake. A paper rewrite audit can support an artifact-level rewrite-dependence claim at conjecture or promising confidence; a full rival implementation is required only when the verdict gates meaningful implementation budget. The rival should be built or reviewed adversarially by someone charged with making it win. Comparative teaching tests use separate groups or counterbalance order to control learning transfer.

Rewrite dependence and changed player responses are separate claims with separate evidence ceilings. An implementation audit can support the former; an interaction sandbox can support the latter only within its tested causal cluster. Political- and epic-channel claims require representative-table evidence and integrated testing at the scope claimed. A null result from an insensitive or underpowered protocol is recorded as no detected effect, not experiential inertness.

## Premise verdict record

Record every evaluated premise under the following four judgments. Each judgment includes its verdict, claim confidence, integration status or `n/a`, evidence pointer, and next discriminator.

- **Structural contribution:** Record rewrite dependence and changed player responses separately, then classify the premise as load-bearing, structurally portable, or unresolved.
- **Fantasy enabling grammar:** Record the rendering's measured or hypothesized effect on learnability, inference, and complexity cost.
- **Experiential effect:** Record effects on learning, inference, and promised epic-channel traces; reserve experientially inert for a sufficiently sensitive representative-table null result.
- **Project selection:** Record provisionally adopt or unsuitable and name every failed constraint, such as complexity budget or genre fit.

A premise is decorative only when it is both structurally portable and experientially inert at the stated scopes and confidence grades. A load-bearing but unsuitable premise remains in a recoverable premise register with its failed constraint, because simplification or rerendering may make it viable later.
